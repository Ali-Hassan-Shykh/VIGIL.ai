System Process

How VIGIL AI Works

From camera input to attendance records — a seamless, intelligent pipeline.

01

Student Face Registration

Each student registers their face through the student portal. Multiple images are captured from different angles and stored securely in the database, creating a unique facial embedding used for future identification.

02

Camera Feed Activation

When a class begins, the teacher activates the monitoring session. The system connects to the classroom camera — whether it's a mobile device, webcam, or CCTV — and begins real-time video analysis.

03

Real-Time Facial Recognition

VIGIL AI's deep learning model continuously scans the video feed, identifying registered students and tracking their presence. Unrecognized individuals trigger immediate security alerts to the admin dashboard.

04

Duration Tracking & Attendance Logic

The system measures how long each detected student remains in the classroom. A student must be present for ≥70% of the class duration for attendance to be automatically marked as "Present". Values below trigger "Absent" or "Short Attendance".

05

Attendance Recorded & Notifications Sent

Once the session ends, attendance is finalized in the database. Students receive instant notifications about their attendance status, allowing them to contact their teacher for corrections if needed.

06

Analytics & Reports Generated

All data is aggregated into comprehensive reports. Admins view institution-wide summaries, teachers see class-level data, and students track their own subject-wise attendance percentages and academic progress.
